  - unwrap non-`-k` multiline subjects into a single logical line,
  - fold long `Subject:` headers,
  - RFC 2047-encode preserved multiline `-k` subjects without splitting quoted-printable escapes.
- Updated `grit/src/commands/am.rs` to decode RFC 2047 Q-encoded subject headers before applying `--keep` / prefix-stripping behavior.
