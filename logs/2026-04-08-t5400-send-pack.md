- **send-pack**: Manual argv parsing (colon refspecs), wire protocol to `sh -c` receive-pack child, side-band pack upload via existing pack-objects helper, strip `GIT_CONFIG_PARAMETERS` from receive-pack so `-c` on send-pack cannot override remote `receive.*` config. - **push**: `--receive-pack` delegates to send-pack path.
- **maintenance**: `run --auto` runs `gc --auto` for `maintenance.strategy=gc` (or unset).
- **gc**: Prune stale `objects/tmp_*` files older than 14 days during `gc` (auto-gc cleanup).
- **trace_packet**: `trace_packet_git_style` for push protocol tests.
- **push receive.denyDeleteCurrent**: Unset key now maps to `Unconfigured` (refuse with message), matching Git.
