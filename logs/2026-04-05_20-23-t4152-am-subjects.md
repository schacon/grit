### Findings so far
- `grit am` currently introduces a blank line in the commit message between wrapped subject lines when reconstructing commit messages from mail input.
- The expected behavior in these tests:
  - with default `am`, wrapped subject should be unwrapped back to a single-line subject;
  - with `am -k`, wrapped subject line breaks should be preserved **without** inserting extra blank lines.

### Fix implemented
- Updated `grit/src/commands/am.rs` mbox parsing flow:
  - Detect and consume leading body lines that are actually folded `Subject:` continuations.
  - When `am` runs in default mode, continuation lines are unwrapped into the subject with spaces.
  - When `am -k` is used, continuation lines are preserved as multiline subject text.
  - Removed the accidental blank-separator insertion between subject continuation lines that previously leaked into commit messages.
